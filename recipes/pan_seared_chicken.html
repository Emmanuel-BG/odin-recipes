Pan-Seared Chicken

Description

One of the healthiest meals, filled with proteins and nutrients, pan seared chicken allows you to quickly cook and eat a delicius meal

This recipe is for 2 people, or someone bulking. Calories are around 400-500.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of raw chicken breat
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a pan to medium-high heat
  2. Salt and pepper the chicken breats
  3. Cook each side for 4 min.
