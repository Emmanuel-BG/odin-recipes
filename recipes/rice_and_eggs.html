Rice and Eggs

Description

This delicious recipe will give you the kick you need to wake up feeling refreshed. Enjoy some rice and eggs today!

This portion is for 2 people. The caloric intake is around 750 for the whole dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 175 grams of uncooked white rice
  • 360 mililiters of water
  • Olive oil
  • 50 grams of turkey ham

Steps

  1. Add the white rice and the water to a saucepan
  2. Boil in high heat until bubbling with lid uncovered
  3. Cover with lid, turn the heat down to lowest
  4. Let cook for 20m
  5. In a bowl, crack the 4 eggs together with sliced ham
  6. Heat a pan with heat on medium
  7. Cook eggs for 2 minutes, stirring frequently
  8. Add cooked riced to pan to mix with eggs
  9. Enjoy!
